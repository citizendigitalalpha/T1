Job profiles

We have profiles on over 800 different types of job, from archivist to zoologist.

Explore job profiles

Each one explains:

  • the skills and qualifications needed to get into that job
  • what the work would be like
  • the pay you could expect
  • what the career prospects are

Job profiles can help you find out what a job involves and if it will suit you.

Do you want

1. A job which is usually based in an office? For example, administrator or accountant
2. A job which usually needs practical skills? For example, a mechanic or hairdresser, a job where you work with your hands to make something
3. To help others? For example, doctor, social worker or carer
4. To work regular, daytime hours? Usually 8am to 4pm or 9am to 5pm on weekdays
5. A job you can get into through an apprenticeship? For example, civil engineer or electrician
6. A job for which you need a qualification from college or university? For example, doctor or dental nurse
7. A job you can train for through a short course (less than six months)? For example, driving instructor or health and safety inspector
